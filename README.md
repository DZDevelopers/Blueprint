# Blueprint
Micro Keyboard / Hackpad 

An 8-key (2×4) mechanical macropad powered by a Seeed Studio XIAO RP2040, designed as a simple and compact hackpad for macros, shortcuts, and experimentation.

This project is fully open-source and includes schematics, PCB design, and a simple enclosure.

Hardware Overview

Microcontroller: Seeed Studio XIAO RP2040

Switches: Cherry MX compatible mechanical switches

LEDs: SK6812 Mini RGB LEDs (per key)

Layout: 2 rows × 4 columns

Use case: Macros, shortcuts, gaming, and productivity

Bill of Materials (BOM)

2 × SK6812 Mini RGB LEDs

8 × Cherry MX mechanical keyboard switches

1 × XIAO-RP2040-DIP microcontroller

1 × Custom-designed PCB

1 × Case (3D-printed)
